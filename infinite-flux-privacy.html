Infinite Flux Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026.

1. Introduction

Infinite Flux ("app", "we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights when you use Infinite Flux.

2. Information We Collect

  • Gameplay progress: scores, streaks, earned runes, upgrade levels, and quest progress stored locally on your device.
  • Diagnostics: device and app diagnostics, usage events, and performance data used to improve stability and gameplay quality.
  • Advertising and consent data: AdMob providers may process device identifiers, consent status, and limited technical information to serve ads and measure performance.

Infinite Flux does not require account creation to play.

3. How We Use Information

  • To save and restore gameplay progress on your device.
  • To operate quests, upgrades, rewards, and core game systems.
  • To improve gameplay balance, stability, and performance.
  • To serve ads and reward-based ad interactions where available.
  • To manage privacy and consent preferences where required.

4. Ads, Tracking, and Storage

Infinite Flux may display third-party ads through Google AdMob. Some ads may be personalized depending on your region, platform settings, and consent choices.

  • Rewarded ads are optional and used only when you choose to watch them.
  • Interstitial ads may appear during gameplay transitions.
  • Gameplay progress is primarily stored locally on your device.
  • Removing the app or clearing app data may delete locally stored progress.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Analytics: app usage and analytics.
  • Google AdMob: advertising and rewarded ads.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: app distribution and platform-managed services.

We do not sell your personal data.

6. Retention, Children, Security, and Contact

  • Locally stored gameplay data remains on your device until you remove it.
  • Analytics and ad-related event data may be retained by service providers under their own policies.
  • Infinite Flux is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children under 13.

We may update this policy from time to time. For privacy questions, contact info@metechi.dev.
