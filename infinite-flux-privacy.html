Infinite Flux Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Infinite Flux (“app”, “we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights when you use Infinite Flux.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Gameplay and Local Progress Data

  • Infinite Flux stores gameplay progress locally on your device, such as scores, streaks, earned runes, upgrade levels, and quest progress.
  • We do not require account creation to play the app.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • We may collect device and app diagnostics, usage events, and performance-related data through analytics tools to improve stability and gameplay quality.

2.3 Advertising and Consent Data

  • Infinite Flux uses Google AdMob to show interstitial and rewarded ads.
  • Ad-related providers may process device identifiers, consent status, and limited technical information to serve ads, measure performance, and comply with privacy requirements.
  • On iOS, we may request App Tracking Transparency permission when required by Apple.

3. How We Use Information

  • To save and restore your gameplay progress on your device.
  • To operate quests, upgrades, rewards, and core game systems.
  • To understand app usage and improve gameplay balance, stability, and performance.
  • To serve ads and reward-based ad interactions where available.
  • To manage privacy and consent preferences where required by law or platform policy.

4. Ads and Tracking

Infinite Flux may display third-party ads through Google AdMob. Some ads may be personalized, depending on your region, your platform settings, and the consent choices you make.

  • Rewarded ads are optional and are used only when you choose to watch them.
  • Interstitial ads may appear during gameplay transitions.
  • If privacy options are required in your region, the app provides a way to review or update your consent choices.

5. Storage

  • Gameplay progress is primarily stored locally on your device.
  • Removing the app or clearing app data may delete locally stored progress that is not backed up by your device or platform.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Analytics: App usage and analytics.
  • Google AdMob: Advertising and rewarded ads.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: App distribution and platform-managed services.

We do not sell your personal data.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Locally stored gameplay data remains on your device until you remove it.
  • Analytics and ad-related event data may be retained by our service providers according to their own retention policies.

8. Children’s Privacy

Infinite Flux is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children under 13.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No system is completely secure, but we continuously work to improve privacy and security practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
