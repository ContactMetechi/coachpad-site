About CoachPad

CoachPad is a mobile and web application that helps coaches, trainers, and teams easily design and animate tactical plans. Users can create drills, organize formations, and share tactical ideas in real time. It’s designed for sports coaches who want a simple yet powerful tactical board tool.

Why We Use Google Sign-In

CoachPad uses Google Sign-In to securely authenticate users and synchronize their tactical data across devices. This ensures that your sessions and animations are safely stored and accessible from any device you log in to. We do not share your personal information with third parties.
