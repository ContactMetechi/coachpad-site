About CoachPad

CoachPad is a mobile and web application that helps coaches, trainers, and teams easily design and animate tactical plans. Users can create drills, organize formations, and share tactical ideas in real time. It is designed for sports coaches who want a simple yet powerful tactical board tool.

Platform and Support

CoachPad is available for iOS and Android, with support and privacy details linked from this page. It is built for coaches, trainers, and teams that need a fast visual workflow for planning and communicating tactics.

Public app listings are available on Google Play and the App Store. Support requests and product inquiries can be sent to info@metechi.dev.
