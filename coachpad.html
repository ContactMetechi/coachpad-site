About CoachPad

CoachPad is a mobile and web application that helps coaches, trainers, and teams easily design and animate tactical plans. Users can create drills, organize formations, and share tactical ideas in real time. It is designed for sports coaches who want a simple yet powerful tactical board tool.

The product focuses on practical coaching workflows: sketching a formation, moving players through an action sequence, explaining a drill before training, and keeping tactical ideas in a format that can be shared with players or staff. The goal is to reduce setup time and make tactical communication easier during preparation and review.
