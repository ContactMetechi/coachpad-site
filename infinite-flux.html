About Infinite Flux

Infinite Flux is a fast-paced mobile arcade game built around lane switching, timing, focus management, and streak control. You dodge hazards, collect shards, build multipliers, complete quests, and push for higher scores in an endless flow.

The game is designed for short, repeatable sessions where reaction speed and decision timing matter. Players manage focus energy, decide when to use Phase mode, and try to keep scoring streaks alive while the run becomes faster and more demanding.

Infinite Flux at a Glance

  • Core loop: switch lanes, avoid hazards, collect shards, and build streaks.
  • Progression: objectives, upgrades, multipliers, and score improvement.

Core Gameplay

  • Switch lanes quickly with taps or swipes.
  • Use Phase mode to pass through danger at the cost of focus energy.
  • Build streaks and multipliers by collecting shards at the right moments.
  • Unlock upgrades and complete daily or weekly objectives.
  • Use optional rewarded ads for a second chance when available.
