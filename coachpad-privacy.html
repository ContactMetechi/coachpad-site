CoachPad Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026.

1. Introduction

CoachPad ("app", "we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights when you use CoachPad.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: email address and a unique user ID when you sign in with email, Google, or Apple.
  • User-created content: tactics, animations, boards, and related content you create in the app.
  • CoachHub data: posts, shared tactics, comments, or profile-related content you upload or share through CoachHub features.
  • Microphone recordings: audio you explicitly record inside the app. CoachPad does not continuously listen in the background.
  • Diagnostics: device model, OS version, crash logs, and usage events used for stability and performance improvements.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide core app functionality and save or sync your content.
  • To authenticate users and manage subscriptions or premium access.
  • To operate CoachHub features and shared content workflows.
  • To support microphone-based features you choose to use.
  • To monitor errors and improve app quality using analytics and crash data.

4. Ads, Storage, and Third-Party Services

CoachPad does not show third-party advertisements in the app.

Local data is stored on your device. If cloud sync is enabled, selected data may be securely stored on our backend.

  • Supabase: authentication and cloud database/sync.
  • Firebase: analytics and crash reporting.
  • Google Play / Apple App Store: in-app purchases and subscription processing.

We do not sell your personal data.

5. Retention, Deletion, Security, and Children

  • Local data can be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.
  • Account and synced cloud data may remain until deletion is requested.
  • CoachPad is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
  • We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure.

6. Changes and Contact

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in the app and/or on this page with a revised last updated date.

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact info@metechi.dev.
