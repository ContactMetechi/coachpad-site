Metechi Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026.

This page summarizes privacy context for the Metechi website. Product-specific privacy pages remain the source of truth for each application.

The Metechi website is a public information site for product descriptions, app availability links, support contact details, privacy references, AI discovery files, and structured product metadata. The website does not replace the product-specific privacy pages linked below.

Website Data

The public Metechi website is an informational portfolio. It links to product pages, app listings, privacy pages, support contact paths, and discovery files.

Visitors can browse the public pages without creating an account on this website. If a user contacts Metechi by email, the submitted email address and message content are used to respond to the request.

AI and Search Discovery

Metechi publishes discovery files such as robots.txt, sitemap.xml, llms.txt, llms-full.txt, ai.txt, an agent card, and an agent skills index so search engines and AI systems can understand the public site structure and product descriptions.
