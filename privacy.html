CoachPad Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 23, 2025

1. Introduction

CoachPad (“app”, “we”, “our”) values the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected, used, shared, and protected when you use the app.

2. Data Collected

2.1. Directly Collected Data

CoachPad does not collect the following user data:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Contact lists

2.2. Automatically Collected Data

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version
  • Usage Data: In-app preferences, settings, created tactics, and animations

2.3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): Device information for ad display (Advertising ID)
  • Firebase: App analytics and crash reporting
  • In-App Purchase: Premium subscription transactions (payment information is handled by Google)

3. Storage and Permissions

Tactics and animations are stored only on your device; no cloud backup or server sync is performed.

Android permissions:

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below)
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO (Android 13+)

4. Ads and Analytics

  • Google AdMob: Test ad units are used; ads are not personalized. Ads can be removed with a premium subscription
  • Firebase: App usage statistics, crash, and performance monitoring

5. Premium Subscription

  • Subscriptions are processed through Google Play Store
  • Premium features: ad-free experience, unlimited PNG/MP4 sharing

6. Data Sharing

User data is not sold or shared with third parties, except for service providers (Google AdMob, Firebase) and legal obligations.

7. Data Security

Tactics and animation data is stored on the device. No internet data transfer is performed (third-party services use SSL encryption).

8. User Rights

  • Data Deletion: Delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Permission Management: Manage permissions in your device settings
  • Subscription Cancellation: Cancel premium subscription via Google Play Store

9. Children’s Privacy

CoachPad is not designed for children under 13 years of age.
