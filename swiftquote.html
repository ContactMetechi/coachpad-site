About SwiftQuote

SwiftQuote is a professional quote and invoice creation app designed for small businesses and freelancers. It helps you create, convert, and share documents quickly while keeping track of your customers and income in one simple dashboard.

The product is intended for people who need to prepare commercial documents without a heavy accounting system. A freelancer can build a quote, convert accepted work into an invoice, share a PDF with a client, and keep customer and product information organized for repeat jobs.

Key Features

  • Quote Creation - Create professional quotes in minutes
  • Quote to Invoice - Convert quotes into invoices with one tap
  • PDF Sharing - Instantly send via WhatsApp or email
  • CRM - Manage customers and product catalogs easily
  • Dashboard - Track income and view statistics
  • 7 Language Support - TR, EN, DE, ES, FR, IT, RU
  • Freemium Model - 5 free documents per month, unlimited with Pro
