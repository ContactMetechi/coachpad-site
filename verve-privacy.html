Privacy Policy for Verve

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Verve. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not use the application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Profile information (photos, bio, prompts)

Media and Device Permissions

  • Camera access (for profile photos and verification)
  • Photo library access (for uploads)
  • Push notifications

Location Data

  • Approximate or precise location (to provide local matches)

Usage Data

  • Likes, passes, matches
  • Chat interactions
  • App activity logs
  • Device and crash analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Match you with other users
  • Enable chat functionality
  • Improve user experience and personalization
  • Prevent fraud and enforce safety features (Report & Block)
  • Send updates and promotional communications (optional)

3. Disclosure of Your Information

Other Users

Your public profile (name, photos, age, location, prompts) is visible to other users of the App. Private messages are only visible to matched users.

Service Providers

  • Supabase – database and authentication infrastructure
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging – push notifications
  • App Store / Google Play – subscription and billing processing

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or if necessary to protect our rights, users, or the public.

4. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no electronic transmission or storage method is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access or update your account information
  • Delete your account at any time from settings
  • Disable location permissions from device settings
  • Opt out of marketing emails

Upon account deletion, your personal data will be permanently removed from active databases within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Verve is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through the App.
